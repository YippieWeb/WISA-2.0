WISA celebrated by the Wellington Airport Community Award

August 2022

WISA was selected as a finalist of the Wellington Airport Community Award for the Educational and child/youth development category. WISA President Jasmine Yip and WISA teacher advisor Mr Ellett attended the award ceremony on 23rd August at the National Library on behalf of the WISA executive team.

WISA 2022 President won the GirlBoss Community Award

September 2022

Jasmine Yip, who is the President of WISA 2022, was acknowledged as one of the ten national winners of the GirlBoss award for her work with WISA. The award was sponsored by Serato and presented by Alexia Hilbertidou, the founder of GirlBoss NZ. Jasmine won the community award, and was interviewed by and featured on Stuff News.

WISA leaders recognised by the Wellington International Student Excellence awards

November 2021

The Mayor of Wellington, Andy Foster, presented awards to 5 WISA leaders - Anh Nguyen, Chinmay Madhusudhan, Jalynne Lee, Jasmine Yip, and Junil Bae - during the Wellington International Student Excellence awards ceremony for their acheivements in Community Service and Leadership.

About Us

Wellington International Students' Association (WISA) is the largest umbrella group that connects international students across high schools in the region. We host numerous events to promote awareness of international culture to our diverse Wellington community, whilst assisting new students integrate into the cosmopolitan community.
