Welcome to WISA

Wellington International Students' Association (WISA) is the largest umbrella group that connects international students across high schools in the region. We host numerous events to promote awareness of international culture to our diverse Wellington community, whilst assisting new students integrate into the cosmopolitan community.

Whanaungatanga

To meet new people, build relationships and make new friends.


Interaction

To participate in events, have fun with each other, and learn who our other international students are around Wellington.


Support

To take care of each other. We value each and everyone around Wellington.


Ako

To both teach and learn about each and everyone's culture.

Our mission is to foster a welcoming and inclusive community for all students, regardless of their cultural background, ethnicity, or nationality. We strive to provide a platform for international students to connect, support, and celebrate diversity through a variety of cultural, educational, and social events.
